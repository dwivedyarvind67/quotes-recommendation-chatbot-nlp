# Business Problem

In today's fast paced digital world many individuals experience stress demotivation emotional imbalance or lack of inspiration due to academic pressure professional challenges and personal issues. Motivational and inspirational quotes help people improve their mental wellbeing and boost positivity.

Currently users rely on manual searching methods such as browsing websites social media or quote collections to find motivational quotes. This process is time consuming inefficient and does not provide personalized results based on the user's emotions.

Traditional quote platforms are static and do not provide conversational interaction with users. As a result users must manually filter through large amounts of content before finding something meaningful.

The main problem is the absence of an intelligent system that can understand a user's mood or intention and recommend relevant quotes instantly.

The Quotes Recommendation Chatbot using NLP and Rasa NLU solves this problem by providing an interactive conversational system that understands user input identifies intent and recommends suitable quotes in real time. This improves accessibility personalization and overall user engagement.
